Essential Baking Tools

Here are the tools every beginner baker should have:

  • Measuring cups & spoons: For accurate measurement of ingredients.
  • Mixing bowls: To mix ingredients more cleaner and easily.
  • Whisk: For mixing eggs, batter, or sauces, ensure it is fully mixed well.
  • Spatula: To fold, scrape, and transfer batter, which prevents overmixing.
  • Baking tray or cake tin: Where you place your dough or batter.
  • Parchment Paper:Prevent the batter or dough to stick on to the tray, making it less messier and eaiser to transfer.
