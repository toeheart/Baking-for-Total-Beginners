Tips & Tricks for Beginners

Here are some tips and tricks that we will share for you as beginner bakers that will help you out:

1

Measurements

Always make sure to measure all the ingreidents accurately, especially for flour and sugar.

2

Preheat the Oven

Most beginner recipes bake at 160°C–180°C. Preheating ensures even cooking and does not waste time.

3

Never overmix

Too much mixing of the batter can cause the baked goods to be dense and flatten during oven.

4

Parchment paper

Most baking batter or dough require a parchment paper, as it prevents it to stick on the tray or tin.

5

Room-temperature ingreidnts

It is recommended to use room-temperature ingredients such as eggs and butter for a smooth mixing.

6

Adjustments

Make adjustments with the taste of sweetness next time when baking, not during baking.

7

Cool Down

Wait until the baked good is completely cool down before cutting and serving.
